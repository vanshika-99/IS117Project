How I Edit My Photos


Cameras I Use:
  • iPhone XS
  • FujiFilm QuickSnap Flash Camera
Apps I Use to Edit:
  • VSCO
  • Unfold
  • Adobe Premiere Pro

There are a lot of different apps that I use to edit my photos, but I mainly use the app VSCO. Editing your photos does not necessarily mean that you have to put filters on it; you can use these apps to adjust the lighting and contrast too. It is also helpful to use these apps to adjust the orientation of your pictures. Sometimes, it is better for me to use an app like VSCO to make sure my photo is not tilted instead of editing it through my Photos app. Below is a few before and after photos of my unedited vs. my edited pictures.
