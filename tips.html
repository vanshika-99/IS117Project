Useful Tips


Tips on Lighting and Angles:
  • Use the Rule of Thirds
  • If taking portraits, make sure your subject is facing the Sun, instead of your camera
  • Use broad lighting, if possible
Helpful Tips:
  • Use landscape mode when necessary
  • Get as close as possible
  • Don't over-use the zoom feature

There are also a few important things to remember for anyone who has just taken up photography. The device you use to take your pictures should not matter. I take most of my pictures on my iPhone XS. Equipment is not as important as making sure your angles and lighting are good. Adjusting the angles and lighting of your photos can make a huge difference in the quality of the picture.
